Electric Program Charge Investment

ABOUT

Energy research and development

The Electric Program Investment Charge (EPIC) program is administered by the California Energy Commission. The goal of the EPIC program is to invest in scientific and technological research that accelerates the state’s energy and climate goals. This project explores different logo designs and the process it took to get to a presentable design.

Challenges we faced

Like all logos, the biggest challenge was attempting to communicate a large amount of information through a concise mark. The design has to perform at both large and small sizes without losing any visual information as well as perform in black & white and color.

The proposed solution

To demonstrate the function of the EPIC program, visual motifs revolving around clean energy must be used. The visual motifs used for these designs were a lightning bolt, a power icon, the letter “E”, and a battery icon.
