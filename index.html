ABOUT

Kristian is a multidisciplinary designer based in Southern California. With an interest in the social impact of design, he pays special attention to the effects visual design has on the human psyche. He has a strong passion for environmentalism and social justice and explores that within his design whenever he can.

Since graduating with a BFA in Graphic Design from Cal Poly Pomona in 2018, he has been working for a clean energy nonprofit. He is currently pursuing a Master of Human-Computer Interaction at Carnegie Mellon University in Pittsburgh, PA.

Aside from design, he loves coffee, dogs, and french fries!
