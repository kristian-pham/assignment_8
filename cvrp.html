Clean Vehicle Rebate Project

ABOUT

Promoting clean vehicle adoption

The Clean Vehicle Rebate Project (CVRP) promotes clean vehicle adoption in California by offering rebates up to $7,000 for the purchase or lease of new, eligible zero-emission vehicles, including electric, plug-in hybrid electric, and fuel cell vehicles.

Challenges we faced

Collectively, cars and trucks account for nearly one-fifth of all US greenhouse gas emissions. Despite this, many Americans refuse to make the switch from vehicles powered by fossil fuels to electric vehicles (EVs) because of outdated perceptions that highlight weaknesses in electric vehicle technology.

The proposed solution

In addition to offering incentives, CVRP also educates consumers about the efficiencies of having an EV. Visually, CVRP takes a friendly approach utilizing illustrations and analogous colors.
