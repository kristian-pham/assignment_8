Water You Doing?

ABOUT

Educating about water pollution

Water You Doing? is an environmental campaign that focuses on educating the public about different types of water pollution. It is easy to look to pin blame on major corporations for detrimental events that wreak havoc on marine life, however, water pollution is a lot closer to home than the average person may think.

Challenges we faced

There are many different types of water pollution that have a multitude of effects on various ecosystems. Every little bit of waste adds up and that is not being seen by the general public. There is a lack of connection between marine ecosystems and the human population, causing ineffective call-to-actions.

The proposed solution

This campaign focuses on placing water pollution in domestic life through the use of a common fishbowl. The fishbowl is an item that is strongly related to domestic life and has the potential to bridge the lack of connection and create empathy.
